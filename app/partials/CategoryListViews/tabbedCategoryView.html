Job fairs and trade shows are the perfect opportunity to brand yourself and generate sales leads and applicants. This booth theme is designed for a fun and inviting environment for attendees. Perfect for summer job recruiting or as an escape from the winter blues.
Promotional items can give the visitors of your booth something to hold on to and will make you memorable. Below you will find items that are effective for both job fairs and trade shows.
Be sure to bring your business cards. If you don’t already have them, you can order them here. Please allow 7-10 business days for delivery.
Collateral materials can help provide information after the visitor has left your booth and can be a valuable tool to bring them back. Some of these pieces are also customizable, so even after the event is over, there is contact info for your office available at their fingertips.
Attracting attention to your booth is a good way to draw a crowd. Having engaging activities will make your booth memorable and possibly bring visitors back. Below you’ll find some recommended activities that are sure to make your booth the hot spot:

  • Photo booth: these photos can be sent to the visitor for them to post to social media sites such as LinkedIn.
  • Buy a small pool and fill with sand. Bury prizes and let visitors dig to find the treasures.
  • Have massage chairs. Give back massages to visitors to relieve “job search stress” or “turnover stress.”
  • Manicures or facials.
  • Have a Frisbee toss. This can be made competitive by adding a Frisbee golf element.
  • Rent a frozen daiquiri machine and pass out virgin drinks in Express cups with beach umbrellas.
